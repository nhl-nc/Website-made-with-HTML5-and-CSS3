Take A Trip

Hello! Many people like to travel. They need some time to prepare before traveling. Everyone's travel budget and travel conditions are different.I will share articles on this web page to prepare trip plans for students and to give tips about these trips.

In general, when we plan a trip, our primary goal is to find a cheap transportation route. The cheap transportation routes and sites that we can use in Europe are as follows, respectively:

  1. Transportation by Plane
    • Ryanair
    • Wizz Air
    • Germanwings
  2. Transportation by Bus
    • Flixbus
    • Eurolines
    • Student Agency Bus
    • Oibus
    • Polski Bus
  3. Transportation information sites
    • Omio
    • Busbud
    • Virail

You can find the cheapest transportation route on the date you want to go by doing research on these sites.

After handling the international transportation, it's time for domestic and urban transportation. I will talk about these in the articles I will share for each country.

As a student, it is more profitable to go to more than one country on each trip, rather than going to a different country each time I go on a trip. I am adding the routes that I and my friends use below in the form of a table.

Route Number First country/city Second country/city Third country/city Fourth country/city Fifth country/city Sixth+ country/city
1 Milano Florence Roma Vatican Pisa Vilnius

3 Paris Brussels Amsterdam Berlin Riga Vilnius

After creating your route, all that remains is to plan the places you will visit. If you have very little time in the city you will visit, you should first make a list of the places you want to go. You can then add or subtract from this list according to their proximity to each other. You can use the Google Maps app to plan your route.

You can use the google maps app offline instead of buying internet in the countries you visit.
